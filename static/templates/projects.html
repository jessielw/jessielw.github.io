Kaleen

About, Kaleen is an international manufacturer of beautiful handmade area rugs. Headquartered in Mumbai, we have various international operations with our United States office and distribution locations in Dalton, Georgia. Kaleen's “Point of Pride & Commitment to Customer Satisfaction” is a reflection of the proven success for over 40 years within the textile industry.

Technologies, Wordpress / PHP / CSS / HTML / JavaScript / jQuery / SQL / Python

Tasks, I was brought on board an existing site to oversee a range of tasks spanning over a year. Below are some highlights of the accomplishments during this period

  • Successfully migrated the server infrastructure from WordPress Engine to a Linux/Nginx setup.
  • Led a small team of developers, ensuring prompt completion of assigned tasks.
  • Implemented extensive optimizations and rewrote a significant portion of the site's code and database queries to accommodate the growing product catalog.
  • Developed custom scripts to accurately import hundreds of thousands of data rows on a monthly basis, thereby keeping the site updated with the latest information.
  • Created a bespoke filter system tailored to the site's inventory of rugs and carpets. The system enables users to filter products based on availability, stock, style, material, construction, color, shape, and size.
